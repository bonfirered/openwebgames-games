Welcome to the PlayCanvas Casino. This is an interactive experiment showing a high resolution 3D environment built with the PlayCanvas Engine. It demonstrates some high-end rendering features:

Physically based rendering, Engine-baked prefiltered reflection probes, Realtime paraboloid reflections, Mesh level of detail, Volumetric fog.

To switch to fly camera, hit the icon in the top right corner. Use the virtual sticks on mobile or WASD and mouse on desktop. Pressing SHIFT makes the camera fly faster.
